## Getting Started

### First Login

1. Open your web browser and navigate to the Employee Leave Tracker URL
2. Enter your username (usually your email address)
3. Enter your password
4. Click "Sign In"

> **First-time users**: You will receive a welcome email with your temporary password. You'll be prompted to change it upon first login.

### Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you'll see your personalized dashboard:

- **Leave Balance**: Current available leave days by type
- **Upcoming Leave**: Your scheduled time off
- **Pending Requests**: Requests awaiting approval
- **Team Calendar**: Visual view of team leave schedule (managers only)
- **Recent Activity**: Latest updates on your requests

### Navigation Menu

- **Dashboard**: Home page with overview
- **My Requests**: View and manage your leave requests
- **New Request**: Submit a new leave request
- **Calendar**: View leave calendar
- **Reports**: Access reports (role-dependent)
- **Settings**: Personal settings and preferences
- **Help**: Access documentation and support

## Employee Guide

### Viewing Leave Balance

1. Your current leave balance is displayed on the dashboard
2. Click on any leave type to see detailed breakdown:
   - **Total Allocation**: Annual entitlement
   - **Used**: Days already taken
   - **Pending**: Days in pending requests
   - **Available**: Days you can still request

### Submitting a Leave Request

#### Step 1: Start New Request

1. Click **"New Request"** or **"Request Leave"** button
2. Select the type of leave:
   - Annual Leave/Vacation
   - Sick Leave
   - Personal Leave
   - Maternity/Paternity Leave
   - Other

#### Step 2: Fill in Details

1. **Start Date**: Select the first day of leave
2. **End Date**: Select the last day of leave
3. **Duration**: Choose full day or half day
   - Morning (9 AM - 1 PM)
   - Afternoon (1 PM - 5 PM)
4. **Reason**: Provide a brief explanation (required for some leave types)
5. **Attachments**: Upload supporting documents if needed (e.g., medical certificates)

#### Step 3: Review and Submit

1. Review the summary:
   - Total days requested
   - Remaining balance after approval
   - Approval workflow
2. Click **"Submit Request"**
3. Confirmation message will appear

### Tracking Request Status

#### Request States

- **Pending**: Awaiting approval
- **Approved**: Request has been approved
- **Rejected**: Request was denied
- **Cancelled**: Request was cancelled by you

#### View Request Details

1. Go to **"My Requests"**
2. Click on any request to see:
   - Current status
   - Approval history
   - Comments from approvers
   - Timeline of actions

### Editing a Request

You can edit pending requests:

1. Go to **"My Requests"**
2. Click on the pending request
3. Click **"Edit"**
4. Update details
5. Click **"Update Request"**

> **Note**: Approved requests cannot be edited. You must cancel and create a new request.

### Cancelling a Request

To cancel a pending or approved request:

1. Go to **"My Requests"**
2. Click on the request
3. Click **"Cancel Request"**
4. Provide a cancellation reason
5. Click **"Confirm Cancellation"**

> **Note**: Cancellation policies may vary by organization. Some organizations require approval for cancellations.

### Viewing Team Calendar

1. Click **"Calendar"** in the navigation
2. View options:
   - **Month View**: See the entire month
   - **Week View**: Detailed week view
   - **Day View**: Single day details
3. Filter by:
   - Department
   - Leave type
   - Team members

### Setting Up Notifications

1. Go to **"Settings"** > **"Notifications"**
2. Choose notification preferences:
   - Email notifications
   - In-app notifications
   - SMS notifications (if enabled)
3. Select what to be notified about:
   - Request status changes
   - Approval required (for managers)
   - Leave reminders
   - Policy updates
4. Save preferences

## Manager Guide

### Manager Dashboard

Additional features on manager dashboard:

- **Team Leave Summary**: Overview of team leave usage
- **Pending Approvals**: Requests awaiting your approval
- **Team Calendar**: Visual representation of team availability
- **Leave Trends**: Analytics on team leave patterns

### Approving Leave Requests

#### View Pending Requests

1. Go to **"Pending Approvals"** or check dashboard
2. You'll see a list of requests with:
   - Employee name
   - Leave type
   - Dates requested
   - Number of days
   - Submission date

#### Review Request

1. Click on a request to open details
2. Review:
   - Employee's leave balance
   - Team coverage during requested period
   - Reason for leave
   - Supporting documents
3. Check team calendar for conflicts

#### Approve or Reject

**To Approve:**
1. Click **"Approve"** button
2. Add comments (optional)
3. Click **"Confirm Approval"**
4. Employee will be notified automatically

**To Reject:**
1. Click **"Reject"** button
2. Provide a clear reason (required)
3. Click **"Confirm Rejection"**
4. Employee will be notified with your reason

#### Delegate Approval

If you're unavailable:
1. Go to **"Settings"** > **"Delegation"**
2. Select a delegate
3. Set delegation period
4. Click **"Save"**

### Managing Team Leave

#### View Team Calendar

1. Click **"Team"** > **"Calendar"**
2. See all team members' leave
3. Filter by:
   - Date range
   - Leave type
   - Employee
4. Export calendar to Excel/PDF

#### Team Leave Reports

1. Go to **"Reports"** > **"Team Reports"**
2. Select report type:
   - Leave utilization
   - Leave patterns
   - Balance summary
3. Choose date range
4. Filter by department/employee
5. Generate and download report

### Managing Coverage

1. Go to **"Team"** > **"Coverage Planner"**
2. View upcoming leave
3. Identify coverage gaps
4. Assign backup personnel
5. Add coverage notes

## HR Admin Guide

### User Management

#### Add New Employee

1. Go to **"Users"** > **"Add User"**
2. Fill in details:
   - Personal information
   - Contact details
   - Department and role
   - Manager assignment
   - Start date
3. Set leave entitlements
4. Click **"Create User"**

#### Import Multiple Users

1. Go to **"Users"** > **"Import"**
2. Download CSV template
3. Fill in employee details
4. Upload completed CSV
5. Map columns
6. Review and import

#### Edit User Details

1. Go to **"Users"** > **"All Users"**
2. Search for user
3. Click **"Edit"**
4. Update information
5. Save changes

#### Deactivate User

1. Find user in **"All Users"**
2. Click **"Deactivate"**
3. Choose deactivation date
4. Decide on leave balance:
   - Forfeit unused leave
   - Payout unused leave
5. Confirm deactivation

### Leave Policy Management

#### Configure Leave Types

1. Go to **"Settings"** > **"Leave Types"**
2. Click **"Add Leave Type"**
3. Configure:
   - Name and description
   - Accrual rate
   - Maximum balance
   - Carryover rules
   - Documentation requirements
4. Save leave type

#### Set Accrual Rules

1. Go to **"Settings"** > **"Accrual Rules"**
2. Define:
   - Accrual frequency (monthly, yearly)
   - Accrual amount
   - Proration rules for new hires
   - Effective dates
3. Apply to employee groups

#### Configure Approval Workflows

1. Go to **"Settings"** > **"Workflows"**
2. Create workflow:
   - Name workflow
   - Add approval steps
   - Set conditions (by leave type, duration, etc.)
   - Assign approvers
3. Activate workflow

### Leave Balance Management

#### Manual Adjustments

1. Go to **"Users"** > **"Leave Balances"**
2. Select employee
3. Choose leave type
4. Click **"Adjust Balance"**
5. Enter:
   - Adjustment amount (+ or -)
   - Reason
   - Effective date
6. Save adjustment

#### Bulk Balance Updates

1. Go to **"Leave Balances"** > **"Bulk Update"**
2. Select:
   - Employee group
   - Leave type
   - Update type (add, set, reset)
3. Specify amount
4. Review changes
5. Apply updates

### Reports and Analytics

#### Generate Reports

1. Go to **"Reports"**
2. Select report type:
   - **Leave Utilization**: Usage by department/employee
   - **Balance Report**: Current balances across organization
   - **Trends Analysis**: Historical patterns
   - **Compliance Report**: Policy adherence
   - **Cost Analysis**: Financial impact of leave
3. Set parameters:
   - Date range
   - Filters (department, employee, leave type)
   - Format (PDF, Excel, CSV)
4. Generate report

#### Schedule Automated Reports

1. Go to **"Reports"** > **"Scheduled Reports"**
2. Click **"New Schedule"**
3. Configure:
   - Report type
   - Frequency (daily, weekly, monthly)
   - Recipients
   - Delivery method
4. Save schedule

### System Configuration

#### General Settings

1. Go to **"Settings"** > **"General"**
2. Configure:
   - Company information
   - Business hours
   - Working days
   - Holidays
   - Time zones
3. Save settings

#### Holiday Calendar

1. Go to **"Settings"** > **"Holidays"**
2. Add holidays:
   - Date

   - Type (public, regional, optional)

#### Email Templates

4. Preview and save

## Common Tasks

### Checking Leave Balance

**Quick Method:**
- Dashboard displays current balance for all leave types

**Detailed Method:**
1. Go to **"My Leave"** > **"Balance Details"**
2. View breakdown by leave type
3. See transaction history
4. Download balance statement

### Requesting Multiple Days

1. Create new request
2. Select start and end dates
3. System automatically calculates:
   - Weekdays (excludes weekends)
   - Non-holiday days
   - Total working days
4. Review and submit

### Requesting Half Days

1. Create new request
2. Select single day
3. Choose duration:
   - **Morning**: Start of day to noon
   - **Afternoon**: Noon to end of day
4. Submit request

### Uploading Documents

For leave types requiring documentation (e.g., sick leave):

1. Create or edit request
2. Scroll to **"Attachments"** section
3. Click **"Upload File"**
4. Select file (PDF, JPEG, PNG)
5. Maximum file size: 5MB
6. Add description (optional)
7. Upload

### Printing Leave Records

1. Go to **"My Requests"**
2. Click **"Print"** or **"Export"**
3. Choose format:
   - PDF for printing
   - Excel for further analysis
4. Select date range
5. Download file

## Tips and Best Practices

### For Employees

1. **Plan Ahead**: Submit requests well in advance
2. **Check Team Calendar**: Avoid conflicts with team members
3. **Provide Details**: Add clear reasons to help approval process
4. **Monitor Balance**: Regularly check your leave balance
5. **Upload Documents**: Attach required documentation promptly
6. **Communication**: Inform team about upcoming leave

### For Managers

1. **Timely Reviews**: Approve/reject requests promptly
2. **Fair Consideration**: Review each request objectively
3. **Clear Communication**: Provide detailed feedback for rejections
4. **Plan Coverage**: Ensure adequate team coverage
5. **Regular Reviews**: Monitor team leave patterns
6. **Set Delegation**: Delegate approval rights when on leave

### For HR Admins

1. **Regular Audits**: Review balances and accruals periodically
2. **Policy Updates**: Keep policies current and communicated
3. **User Training**: Provide training for new users
4. **Data Backup**: Regularly backup leave records
5. **Compliance**: Ensure adherence to labor laws
6. **System Maintenance**: Keep system updated

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on login page, enter your email, and follow the reset link.

**Q: Can I request leave for past dates?**
A: Generally no, but HR can enter retroactive leave with proper approval.

**Q: What happens to my unused leave?**
A: Depends on your organization's policy (carryover, payout, or forfeit).

**Q: Can I cancel approved leave?**
A: Yes, but cancellation may require approval depending on organization policy.

**Q: How are weekends counted?**
A: Weekends are automatically excluded from leave day calculations.

**Q: Do public holidays count as leave days?**
A: No, public holidays don't count against your leave balance.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions).

**Q: Can I access the system on mobile?**
A: Yes, the system is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures.

**Q: Can I sync with my calendar?**
A: Yes, calendar integration is available (Google Calendar, Outlook).

### Policy Questions

**Q: How much leave am I entitled to?**
A: Check your dashboard or contact HR for your specific entitlements.

**Q: Can I take unpaid leave?**
A: Yes, if permitted by your organization's policy.

**Q: What if I'm sick and can't submit a request?**
A: Contact your manager immediately; they can submit on your behalf or you can submit when able.

**Q: Can I transfer leave to another employee?**
A: Generally no, but check with HR for organization-specific policies.
